Practitioners are making AI decisions in real practices, with real clients, without a framework. The liability, the ethical risk, and the clinical consequences land entirely on them. CLEARED exists because that conversation needed to happen, and nobody was having it.

The Red / Yellow / Green Zone Framework
Red Zone
AI does not belong here.
Any output that directly influences a specific client's care.
Yellow Zone
AI can support here with defined practitioner review.
These require full practitioner review before anything moves forward.
Green Zone
AI can operate here with minimal oversight.
Content drafts, scheduling, general research.
The Human Authority Filter

Every AI output that touches a client must pass through the practitioner's judgment before it reaches the client. The practitioner is the authority. Always. Governance is not about restricting AI. It is about positioning the practitioner correctly so that authority remains structural, not optional.
